Comprehensive Health Monitoring and First-Aid Diagnostics

Our innovative diagnostic kit for domestic animals, coupled with a user-friendly mobile app and website, offers comprehensive health monitoring and first-aid diagnostics. Key features include Skin Cancer, ECG monitoring, blood oxygen level tracking, species classification via image capture, noise-filtered data analysis, and telehealth integration for emergency consultations. Additionally, an IVR system caters to users without internet access or smart devices. This solution provides immediate diagnostics, enhances accessibility to care, offers emergency support, ensures convenience, and promotes inclusivity.
FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How does the diagnostic kit work? The diagnostic kit uses sensors to monitor vital signs and ML algorithms for early disease detection.
Can the app be used for multiple animals? Yes, the app can be used to monitor the health of multiple domestic animals.
Is the data analysis accurate? The ML model filters noise from readings to provide accurate data analysis.
How can I connect with a veterinarian for consultations? You can connect with veterinarians through video calls and real-time chat via the telehealth integration feature.
What languages are supported by the IVR system? The IVR system provides guidance in local languages for areas without stable internet or smart devices.
